Our Story

Built on Showing Up

A family-driven company serving the Niagara Region with professional property care — every season, no excuses.

How It Started

From Building Homes to Building a Business

Steadfast started as Steadfast Building Group — a vision my dad and I shared after years of working in the home construction industry. We wanted to build something of our own. When my family eventually moved to Manitoba, I stayed, kept the name, and redirected the business toward what I knew I could do well.

It started with pressure washing. Then came landscaping. Then snow removal. Four years later, Steadfast Home Services is a recognized name in the Niagara Region — trusted by homeowners, businesses, and even the Municipality of Niagara-on-the-Lake, who we've now served for three consecutive contract years.

What Steadfast Means

Always Showing Up. Always Delivering.

The name isn't just branding. Steadfast means doing what needs to be done — no matter the time, no matter what's going on in life. When customers rely on you, you show up. That's it.

What drives this business is simple: building a life where I can provide for my family and give them security. That motivation is present in every job we take on. When your property looks good, that matters to us — not just because it's our job, but because it reflects the standard we hold ourselves to.

Who We Serve

Homeowners, Businesses & Municipalities

The majority of our customers are homeowners across the Niagara Region who want reliable, quality property care without having to think about it. But we also serve commercial clients — including the Municipality of Niagara-on-the-Lake, who have renewed their contract with us year after year.

Whether you're a homeowner who wants a clean lawn all season or a business that needs dependable snow removal, we bring the same standard of work to every property we touch. We're actively growing both sides of the business — residential and commercial.

The Experience

You Shouldn't Have to Follow Up

The goal is simple: you hire us, and you stop worrying about your property. We don't want you calling us to fix something. We don't want you second-guessing whether the job was done right. We want you to look at your property and feel proud of it.

We care about your property the way you would. That means not taking shortcuts, not rushing through jobs, and being honest with you. You're trusting us with your home or business — we take that seriously.

The Reality of This Business

What Most People Don't See

People see a crew show up and mow a lawn. What they don't see is everything behind it.

Professional landscaping equipment alone runs close to $10,000. Then there's the vehicles, fuel, insurance, taxes, software, and the time it takes to run an operation properly. When you hire Steadfast, you're not just paying for a cut — you're investing in a professional, insured, accountable service that shows up reliably and stands behind its work.

We believe in being upfront about that. It's part of treating our customers with respect.
