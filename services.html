Lawn Care

Lawn Mowing & Edging

A well-maintained lawn starts with consistent, professional cuts. We offer weekly or bi-weekly mowing schedules to keep your grass looking its best throughout the season.

Every cut is done at a 3-inch height using our professional Toro equipment — the right height to keep your lawn healthy, thick, and resistant to weeds. We don't rush. Every visit is thorough.

Weed whacking along fences, obstacles, and tight areas is included in every visit, as is clean edging along all garden beds. You'll come home to a property that looks sharp — not just mowed.

What's Included

  • Weekly or bi-weekly mowing schedule
  • Professional cut at 3-inch height
  • Toro commercial-grade equipment
  • Weed whacking around all obstacles
  • Garden bed edging
  • Debris cleanup after each visit

Snow Removal

Snow Plowing & Salting

When a snowfall hits 2 cm or more, we're already on it — 24 hours a day, throughout the entire snow season. You won't have to set an alarm or worry about your driveway. We handle it before you need to leave.

Every property is hand shovelled by default. We believe in doing the job right and respecting your property. If you prefer us to use a plow or snow blower, that option is always available — but we leave that choice up to you.

After clearing, we apply a quality ice melt to keep your surfaces safe. We use the right amount — not more. Over-salting damages driveways and is hard on the environment, so we're deliberate about how we apply it.

What's Included

  • Automatic trigger at 2 cm+ snowfall
  • 24/7 service throughout snow season
  • Hand shovelling on all properties (default)
  • Plow or snow blower available on request
  • Quality ice melt / salting after every clear
  • Driveway and front walkway coverage

Seasonal Care

Spring Cleanup

After a long winter, your property needs a fresh start. Our spring cleanup service is a comprehensive reset — clearing out everything the cold season left behind and getting your lawn and gardens ready for summer.

We go through every garden bed: cleaning out dead growth, pulling weeds, and redefining clean, sharp edges. Your full grass area gets raked and blown so it looks fresh from the first warm day of the year.

By the time we leave, your property will look like it's been cared for all winter — because now it will be.

What's Included

  • Full garden bed cleaning and weeding
  • Garden bed edge redefinition
  • Complete lawn rake and blow
  • Removal of all fall and winter debris
  • Property left clean and ready for the season

Seasonal Care

Fall Cleanup

As the season winds down, your property needs one final round of attention before winter sets in. We start fall cleanups around October, working with the natural rhythm of the season as leaves begin to fall.

We collect and remove all leaves, pull remaining weeds, and tidy up your beds. If you need branches trimmed before the snow arrives, we can take care of that too.

A proper fall cleanup means a healthier lawn in spring. Leaving debris over winter traps moisture, invites disease, and smothers new growth. We make sure your property goes into the cold months in good shape — so it comes back stronger.

What's Included

  • Full leaf collection and removal
  • Garden bed weed removal
  • Branch trimming (on request)
  • General property tidy before winter
  • Available starting October
