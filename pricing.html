Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to sign a contract?

A contract is required for the Year-Round Bundle (snow + lawn care). Individual seasonal plans — lawn care only or snow removal only — do not require one.

What happens if it snows more than once in a day?

We come back as many times as it snows. If your property gets hit two or three times in one day, we'll be there every time. Your driveway and walkways stay clear no matter how many times the snow comes down.

What triggers a snow removal visit?

Any snowfall of 2 cm or more triggers a visit. We monitor conditions closely so you never have to worry about timing — we're on it before you even wake up.

Can I cancel my plan?

You can cancel a non-contract plan at any time. Please note that you will be billed for the remaining portion of your current month depending on your cancellation date.

Do you service my area?

We serve the Niagara Region. If you're unsure whether we cover your specific street or neighbourhood, reach out and we'll let you know.

What equipment do you use?

We use Toro zero-turn mowers and commercial-grade string trimmers for clean, consistent results on every visit.

How do I pay?

We accept payment by debit card or e-transfer. Simple and hassle-free.

What if I'm not happy with the service?

Your satisfaction matters to us. Get in touch and we'll do whatever it takes to make it right.
